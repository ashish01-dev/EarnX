## Brand & Style
The design system is engineered for **EarnX**, an ultra-premium fintech platform at the intersection of the creator economy and luxury Web3 finance. The brand personality is authoritative yet visionary, evoking the feeling of a high-end digital private bank.

The chosen style is **Cinematic Glassmorphism**. This aesthetic combines the structural discipline of high-end SaaS (Stripe/Linear) with the immersive depth of modern cinematic interfaces. It leverages heavy background blurs, layered translucency, and vibrant "plasma" light sources to create a sense of physical space within a digital screen. Visuals are defined by high-contrast typography, motion-blurred accents, and a meticulous attention to lighting and micro-interactions.

## Colors
The palette is rooted in deep obsidian tones to provide a "limitless" canvas for light. 
- **Primary Background (#0f0e17)**: A near-black void that allows glow effects to pop.
- **Glowing Orange (#ff8906)**: The core action color, used for "magnetic" elements and high-priority interactions.
- **Secondary & Tertiary Accents**: Used sparingly for data visualization and status indicators, often presented as gradients.
- **Text Hierarchy**: Pure white (#fffffe) is reserved for headlines to maximize contrast, while a muted silver-blue (#a7a9be) handles dense body information to reduce eye strain.

Apply a **0.03 opacity noise texture** over all backgrounds to break digital flatness and add a premium, filmic grain.

## Typography
Typography follows a "Massive & Minimal" philosophy. 
- **Headlines**: Syne provides a distinctive, ultra-modern geometric flair. Display sizes should use "ExtraBold" or "Bold" with tight tracking to create a high-impact, editorial look.
- **Body**: Plus Jakarta Sans offers exceptional legibility with a soft, friendly touch that balances the aggressive headlines.
- **Technical Data**: JetBrains Mono is used for transaction IDs, wallet addresses, and secondary labels to reinforce the Web3/fintech utility.
- **Scaling**: On mobile, display sizes should aggressively scale down, but maintain their heavy weight to preserve brand character.

## Layout & Spacing
The layout system utilizes an **Interactive Bento Grid** model. This involves modular, card-based containers of varying sizes that snap to a 12-column grid.

- **Desktop**: 12-column grid, 24px gutters, 80px margins. Content is organized into "island" clusters.
- **Mobile**: Single column with 16px margins. 
- **Philosophy**: Use generous whitespace (section gaps of 120px+) to allow the "floating" elements to breathe. Alignment should be razor-sharp, mimicking the precision of high-end physical architecture. Components should feel "heavy" and grounded by their internal padding.

## Elevation & Depth
Depth is not communicated through traditional shadows, but through **light and translucency**.

- **Layers**: 
  - **Level 0 (Base)**: Darkest background with animated mesh gradients (orbs of #e53170 and #ff8906 at 5% opacity).
  - **Level 1 (Cards)**: Glassmorphism surfaces. Use `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)` and a semi-transparent dark fill (`rgba(22, 22, 26, 0.6)`).
  - **Level 2 (Modals/Popovers)**: Higher blur (40px) and a subtle inner glow on the top edge to simulate a catch-light.
- **Stroke Depth**: Every interactive card must have a 1px border. Use a linear gradient stroke from `rgba(255,255,255,0.2)` to `rgba(255,255,255,0.05)` to simulate a reflective edge.
- **Glows**: Use radial gradients behind primary buttons to create a "bloom" effect on the dark surface below.

## Shapes
This design system uses a **Pill-shaped (Level 3)** language to echo the smoothness of luxury hardware. 
- **Standard Cards**: 24px - 32px corner radius.
- **Buttons & Navbars**: Full pill-shape (radius: 9999px).
- **Interactive States**: When hovered, cards should subtly "inflate" (scale 1.02x) with a spring-based animation. 
The softness of the corners provides a necessary counter-balance to the sharp, technical typography.

## Components
- **Magnetic Buttons**: Primary buttons use a `#ff8906` to `#f25f4c` gradient. On hover, implement a magnetic pull effect where the button moves slightly toward the cursor, accompanied by a concentrated "bloom" glow.
- **Glassmorphism Navbar**: A floating pill-shaped bar anchored to the top or bottom. It should use maximum backdrop blur and a thin white border.
- **Bento Cards**: Interactive containers with "floating reflections" (a diagonal white gradient that moves across the card on mouse-over).
- **Input Fields**: Minimalist. Only a bottom border that transforms into a full glowing orange outline upon focus.
- **Scrollbars**: Ultra-thin, 4px wide, using the tertiary accent color with a soft blur.
- **Mesh Gradients**: Backgrounds should feature 2-3 large, slow-moving blurred orbs that shift position based on scroll progress (parallax).
